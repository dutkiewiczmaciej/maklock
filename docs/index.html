Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for privacy-conscious Mac users who want real app protection — not security theater.

Touch ID

Single fingerprint prompt unlocks instantly. No double authentication, no clunky password dialogs.

Apple Watch

Apps unlock automatically when your Watch is on your wrist and nearby. BLE wrist detection — no other app locker has this.

Lock on App Switch

Re-locks every time you switch to a protected app. Not just on launch — on every activation. No gaps in protection.

Close on Sleep

Close your laptop — messaging apps are terminated instantly. Nobody can snoop your conversations later.

Auto-Close Inactive

Protected apps close after a configurable timeout. Per-app control — keep your editor open, close your messengers.

Multi-Monitor

Blur overlay covers all connected displays simultaneously. No peeking at content on the other screen.

How It Works

Set up in seconds.

MakLock runs silently in your menu bar. No Dock icon, no distractions.

1

Add your apps

Choose which apps to protect. Toggle per-app settings like auto-close for messaging apps.

2

Apps get locked

When anyone opens or switches to a protected app, a blur overlay instantly blocks all content on every monitor.

3

Authenticate

Touch ID, Apple Watch on your wrist, or password. Re-locks on idle, sleep, or when your Watch leaves range.
